Who is Sam Altman?

Born on April 22, 1985, Sam Altman is a leading figure in the AI boom, an American entrepreneur, and investor who has significantly shaped the technology landscape.

As the CEO of OpenAI, Altman leads the organization responsible for developing groundbreaking AI systems like ChatGPT, which have revolutionized how we interact with artificial intelligence.

Before OpenAI, Altman served as the president of Y Combinator, Silicon Valley's premier startup accelerator, where he nurtured successful companies like Airbnb, Dropbox, and Reddit, helping transform them into billion-dollar enterprises.

Beyond his operational roles, Altman is known for his visionary thinking on the responsible development of artificial intelligence and his advocacy for ensuring that advanced AI benefits humanity as a whole.
