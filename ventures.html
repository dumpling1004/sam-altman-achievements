Other Ventures & Investments

Investment Portfolio Overview

Beyond his leadership roles at Y Combinator and OpenAI, Sam Altman has built an impressive investment portfolio that spans multiple industries and technologies. As of June 2024, his investments include stakes in over 400 companies with a combined valuation of approximately $2.8 billion.

Altman's investment strategy demonstrates his forward-thinking approach and commitment to technologies that can transform industries and address global challenges. His portfolio reflects his interests in artificial intelligence, clean energy, biotechnology, transportation, and consumer technology.

Featured Investments

Helion Energy

Sam Altman committed $375 million to Helion Energy and serves as the company's chairman. Helion is working on developing fusion energy technology, which could provide clean, abundant, and affordable electricity.

Retro Biosciences

With a $180 million investment, Altman is backing Retro Biosciences' mission to extend human healthspan by a decade. The company focuses on cellular reprogramming, autophagy, and plasma-inspired therapeutics.

Reddit

As an early investor in Reddit, Altman has supported one of the internet's most influential community platforms. He also briefly served as CEO during a transitional period for the company.

Stripe

Altman was an early investor in Stripe, which has become one of the world's most valuable fintech companies, providing payment processing infrastructure for internet businesses.

Oklo Inc.

Investing in advanced nuclear technology, Altman has backed Oklo's development of small nuclear reactors that could provide clean, reliable energy with minimal waste.

Boom Technology

Altman has invested in Boom, which is developing supersonic commercial aircraft to make high-speed travel accessible, economical, and sustainable.
